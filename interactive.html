A/D/O Before and After

At the ProPublica Data Institute, we learned how to make an interactive photo that toggles between two images when clicked. Pictured below is the Greenpoint, Brooklyn, design-focused makerspace A/D/O before and after its construction was finished. A/D/O now hosts Urban-X, a business accelerator for hardware companies whose products improve city living, as well as many events focused upon design and technology.
